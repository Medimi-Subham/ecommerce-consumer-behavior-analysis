# E-Commerce Consumer Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview



This project analyzes consumer purchasing behavior in an e-commerce environment using SQL Server and Power BI.



The objective is to identify customer segments, shopping patterns, marketing influences, and loyalty drivers that impact purchasing decisions and revenue generation.



### Tools Used

* SQL Server
* SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS)
* Power BI
* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics



Business Objectives

1. Analyze customer demographics
2. Understand shopping behavior
3. Evaluate marketing effectiveness
4. Measure customer loyalty impact
5. Identify revenue-driving customer segments



### **The dataset includes:**



* Customer Demographics
* Income Level
* Occupation
* Education Level
* Purchase Amount
* Purchase Frequency
* Payment Method
* Purchase Channel
* Product Rating
* Customer Satisfaction
* Loyalty Membership
* Brand Loyalty
* Advertisement Engagement
* Purchase Intent



#### SQL Analysis Performed

* Revenue Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Demographic Analysis
* Loyalty Analysis
* Marketing Impact Analysis
* Shopping Behavior Analysis





### Dashboard Pages



1. **Executive Summary**



Key business KPIs and revenue insights.



**2. Customer Analysis**



Demographic and customer segment analysis.



**3. Consumer Behavior Analysis**



Purchase patterns and shopping preferences.



**4. Loyalty & Marketing Analysis**



Impact of loyalty programs, satisfaction, and marketing engagement on spending behavior.



### Key Insights

1. Customer spending varies across demographic segments.
2. Loyalty membership does not always guarantee higher spending.
3. Advertisement engagement influences purchasing behavior.
4. Customer satisfaction and brand loyalty affect purchase value.
5. Different shopping channels and payment methods show varying adoption patterns.
